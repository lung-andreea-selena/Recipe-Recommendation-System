Cooking smarter, wasting less

Recipe4U is a smart web application designed to assist users in discovering relevant meals based on the ingredients they already have, while also aiming to reduce food waste.

The application relies on a content-based filtering approach and integrates Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to preprocess and match user input with recipes from a structured dataset.

Food waste has become a critical global concern, even at the household level because a significant proportion of edible food is unnecessarily discarded. Motivated by this, Recipe4U aims to empower users to make the most of their ingredients and minimize waste.
