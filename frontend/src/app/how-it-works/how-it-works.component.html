So how does Recipe4U work?

Start on the Home page by browsing the Available Ingredients list. Tap the add icon next to an item to move it into your personal pantry. You’ll need to choose at least three ingredients before any recipes can be shown.

Every ingredient you pick is displayed in the Selected Ingredients card on the right. If you change your mind, just tap the remove icon beside an ingredient to take it out. Looking for something specific? Use the search bar above the lists to filter ingredients as you type.

When you’re happy with your selection, click Show me recipes. In a few seconds the results appear, sorted by how closely they match what’s already in your kitchen. Recipes that require no extra ingredients are listed first so you can start cooking right away. After the zero-extra list, you’ll see dishes that need one or more additional items.

Curious about a dish? Click on any recipe card to open its full details. You’ll get step-by-step directions and a link to the original recipe, along with the source.

Happy cooking! :)
